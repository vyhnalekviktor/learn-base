Mint Your NFT

Create your first on-chain NFT on Base Sepolia testnet

Minting an NFT means creating a unique digital token on the blockchain. When you mint, a smart contract generates a new token with a specific ID that becomes yours forever.

Each NFT from this collection has a randomly generated color and is stored entirely on-chain - no external servers needed!

The minting process is simple:

  1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet)
  2. Make sure you're on Base Sepolia testnet
  3. Click the "Mint NFT" button below
  4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
  5. Wait 1-2 seconds for confirmation

Note: You'll need a small amount of Base Sepolia testnet ETH for gas fees. Get it free from Alchemy Faucet.

After successfully minting your NFT:

  • The NFT appears in your wallet's NFT/Collectibles section
  • You can view it on BaseScan
  • The transaction is permanently recorded on Base blockchain
  • You own this unique digital asset with a one-of-a-kind color

Your NFT is an SVG image with randomly generated color stored entirely on-chain!
