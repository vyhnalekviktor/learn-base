How to Send Funds on Base

Guide to your first transaction on Base

You need USDC and ETH on Base for transactions. USDC is what you'll send, while ETH pays for gas fees (transaction costs). Gas fees on Base are extremely cheap - typically $0.001 - $0.01.

Try sending a small amount of USDC to another address. This simulates a real transfer.
